FAQ

How many team members can I invite?

You can invite 3 team members as maximum.

What is the maximum file upload size?

No more than 2GB. All files in your account must fit your allotted storage space.

How do I reset my password?

Firstly, you have to add your phone number or gmail address, where you'll get a verification code and after filling that code in a right blank, you can reset your password then, but remember: password must be at least 6 characters long and must contain a number!

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription any time you want.

Do you provide additional support?

Yes, we provide additional support and you can send us a message or feedback any time you want.
